Menate Associaton Cup

Article Talk

The Menate Association Cup, formerly known as Menate Cup, is an annual football tournament in Menate.First played during the 1956 season, it is the first and the oldest football tournament in Menate.The competititon was previously managed by the Menate Football Federation (MFF), before it was taken over by the new Menate Football Association (MFA) in 1966.
